PACMOCHI

A GENLAYER TALE

In the year 404 PG, the digital realm splintered into countless Layers. Dungeons of dumb consensus, patrolled by validator golems trapped in their own code.

From a glitch in a forgotten testnet rose PacMochi. The first wizard with a brain. Where others execute, he reasons. Where others follow paths, he reshapes them.

Collect the Genesis Coins. Outwit the golems. Reach the Vault, and remember the name of the network that thinks.

"Some chains just execute. PacMochi thinks."
